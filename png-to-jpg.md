## Definition
The PNG to JPG Converter is a utility that transforms images from the Portable Network Graphics (PNG) format to the Joint Photographic Experts Group (JPG/JPEG) format. PNGs are known for lossless compression and transparency support, while JPGs are known for high compression and smaller file sizes, making them ideal for photographs. When converting, this tool automatically fills any transparent areas in the PNG with a white background, as JPG does not support transparency. This is essential for reducing file sizes for web use.
