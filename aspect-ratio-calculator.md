## Definition
The Aspect Ratio Calculator helps you find the missing dimension of an image or video while maintaining a specific aspect ratio. An aspect ratio is the proportional relationship between the width and height of a display or image (e.g., 16:9 for HD video). If you know the width and the ratio, this tool calculates the height, and vice versa. It's crucial for resizing content without distortion.

### What is 16:9?
It's the standard widescreen shape for TVs, YouTube, and most laptops.

### How do I calculate aspect ratio from dimensions?
Divide width by height. 1920 / 1080 = 1.77, which is 16:9.
