## Definition
The JPG to PNG Converter transforms images from JPG/JPEG format to PNG. While JPG is great for photos, PNG is a lossless format that is better for editing, as it doesn't degrade quality each time you save it. This tool takes your compressed JPG and saves it as a high-quality PNG. Note that this won't magically restore quality lost during the original JPG compression, but it prevents further loss during future edits.
