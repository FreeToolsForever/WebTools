## FAQ
### Does this count spaces as characters?
Yes! I show you both 'Characters (with spaces)' and 'Characters (without spaces)' so you have all the data you need.

### Is there a word limit?
No. You can paste a whole book if you want. I process it all instantly in your browser.

### Does it count numbers as words?
Yes. '100' counts as one word. 'One hundred' counts as two words.

### How is reading time calculated?
I assume an average reading speed of 200 words per minute. It's a good estimate for most blog posts and articles.

### Is my text saved?
Never. Your text stays in your browser's memory. As soon as you close the tab, it's gone forever.

### Does it work with other languages?
Yes! I work with any language that uses spaces to separate words.

### Can I use this offline?
Yes. Once you load this page, you can disconnect from the internet and keep writing.
